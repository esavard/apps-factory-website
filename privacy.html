Apps Factory ("we", "our", or "us", "Étienne Savard") operates a portfolio of mobile applications. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Our core philosophy is to minimize data collection. The specific data collected depends on the application you are using:

  • Offline Applications: Many of our utility applications operate 100% offline. They do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: User settings (such as UI themes or game progress) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Some of our applications may use third-party services to support development. These services may collect non-identifiable information:

  • Advertising: Free versions of our apps may use third-party ad networks (such as AppLovin or Google AdMob). These networks may use device identifiers to provide relevant advertisements.
  • In-App Purchases: We use Google Play Billing to process premium upgrades (e.g., ad removal). We do not process, collect, or have access to your payment details.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
