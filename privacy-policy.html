1. Information We Collect

Our utility applications are designed to function primarily offline. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, to support our development through advertising, we use third-party services (specifically AppLovin) that may collect certain information automatically when you use the app:

  • Device and Usage Information: Device IDs (such as Android Advertising ID), device type, operating system version, and app interaction data.
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from your IP address to serve localized advertisements.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected automatically by our third-party SDKs is used strictly for:

  • Advertising: To serve targeted advertisements via the AppLovin network.
  • Analytics: To monitor app performance, identify crashes, and ensure the stability of our applications.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. The data collected by our advertising partners is shared directly with their ad networks to facilitate the bidding and serving of ads. For more details on how our advertising partner handles your data, please review the AppLovin Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our applications. Since our applications operate entirely locally on your device (outside of the advertising SDK network requests), your locally generated operational data (such as generated passwords) is never transmitted over the internet or stored on our servers.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region (e.g., Canada, EEA, UK, US states), you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Because we do not store your personal data on our servers, requests related to advertising tracking should be managed directly through your device settings (e.g., by resetting your Advertising ID or opting out of personalized ads in your Android system settings).

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information via our third-party SDKs, please contact us so we can take appropriate measures.
